CARDSAGAINSTHUMANITY

  • Cards Against Humanities is a card game that gets as awkward and despicable as you and your friends can make it.


    The game is simple. Each round, the card tzar will read out one black card, and everyone else will try to match with their funniest white card.

  • Example

  • For example, say the following is a card that the tzar has read aloud the black card. The other players would then play white cards accordingly, and after they are all submitted, the tzar would then pick the card they think is the funniest.

  • How am I maintaining my relationship status?

  • Starting a game

  • To start a game, just click on the button to the left. You can then invite your friends to play with you just by giving the URL to them. A game can start when there are at least three players.
